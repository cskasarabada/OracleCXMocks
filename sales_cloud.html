Stage 5: Closure – 100% Won or 0% Lost

Closeout moments are where CX Sales Cloud hands the baton to downstream delivery teams. Use this view to reinforce how the combined Lead → Opportunity → Quote → Pursuit → Project experience captures every lesson learned.

Closed Won

  • Contract is signed, automation springs into action to generate orders and downstream PPM projects.
  • Automatic order creation for processing, project management kickoff, and customer handoffs.
  • Project templates instantiated for delivery teams with resource notifications already queued.
  • Revenue recognition workflows initiated and tied to proposal commitments.
  • Customer onboarding assistants triggered to capture success criteria and warranty expectations.

Closed Lost

  • Capture detailed win/loss intelligence to refine approach and strengthen future proposals.
  • Log competitor analysis, positioning gaps, and pricing or terms concerns.
  • Track product fit requirements or unmet milestones across pursuits.
  • Document timing or budget constraints that blocked advancement.

Gate Criteria – Quality Checkpoints

Each stage requires specific gate criteria before progression, minimizing risk and increasing forecast accuracy.

Early-Stage Gates

  • Customer interest validated and relationship strengthened.
  • Budget & timeline confirmed during Qualification.
  • SME review completed for Feasibility.
  • Ops / engineering checkpoint complete for Scope Definition.

Mid-Stage Gates

  • Finance + Ops approval finalized prior to Pre-Bid.
  • Proposal submitted with disposition plan.
  • Customer feedback received during Client Review.

Late-Stage Gates

  • Commercial alignment confirmed in Negotiation.
  • Contract finalized for Award/Contracting.
  • Disposition confirmed (Closed Won or Closed Lost).
