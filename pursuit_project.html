Pursuit Project

The connected preconstruction workspace

In Oracle PPM, Pursuit Projects capture preconstruction effort, resource scheduling, and decision data before work is awarded. Paired with CX Sales, they allow Argano clients like Miron to pass intelligence cleanly into construction delivery.

What it is

  • Overhead PPM project linked to an Opportunity
  • Tracks internal labor, tasks, and cost-only effort
  • Temporary record that transitions to construction

Preconstruction Workspace

  • Conceptual estimating & value engineering
  • Engineering reviews, site investigations
  • Design alternatives and early schedules

Resource Management

  • Request estimators, engineers, PMs
  • View availability (6 booked, 2 open, 1 with right skill)
  • Balance workloads before award
Cost Insight Capture internal spend per pursuit to analyze win/loss ratio, cost per pursuit, and estimating productivity.
Decision Engine Opportunity stages trigger Pursuit creation, feeding a “thinking → doing” handoff with structured WBS tasks.
Lifecycle Link When awarded, the Pursuit becomes the front half of the project. Construction project inherits the same Program for full reporting.
