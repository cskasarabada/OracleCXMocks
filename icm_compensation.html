Plan Components
Component 1
Base Commission

5% of revenue on all closed-won deals within assigned territory. Applies from $0 to 100% of quota attainment at progressive rates (4.5% below 80%, 5.5% at 80–100%).

Component 2
Accelerator Bonus

9% commission rate applies for revenue credited between 100% and 120% of quota. Above 120% quota, rate escalates to 13.5% — a 2.5× multiplier on the base rate.

Component 3
Product SPIFs & Kickers

+$500 per ICM module sold. +$750 per CPQ deal closed. Kickers are paid in the same pay cycle as the associated transaction and do not count against quota revenue.

Component 4
Management Override

2% override on all direct reports' credited revenue. Applied quarterly after individual rep payouts are finalized. Eligible for up to 3 direct reports in Q4.

Component 5
Retention Bonus

$15,000 year-end retention bonus payable if 110%+ of annual quota is achieved by December 31, 2024, and employment remains active at date of payment (Jan 15, 2025).

Territory & Eligibility Rules

  • TerritorySouthwest — Arizona (AZ), California (CA), Nevada (NV), New Mexico (NM), Utah (UT)
  • Product LinesOracle CX Suite (Sales Cloud, CPQ, ICM, Service Cloud, Commerce)
  • Eligible ProductsOracle Sales Cloud, Oracle CPQ, Oracle ICM, Oracle Service Cloud
  • Deal OwnershipPrimary rep on opportunity at close. Split credits permitted with manager approval.
  • Credit TimingRevenue credited upon contract execution and Oracle order entry confirmation.
  • Clawback Policy100% clawback within 60 days if deal cancels. 50% clawback at 61–120 days.
  • Pay CycleMonthly payout — 15th of the month following credit date. Q4 final true-up Jan 15, 2025.
Dispute Process: To dispute a transaction, click the Dispute button on the Transactions tab. Include the deal name, amount, and reason for dispute. Disputes are reviewed by your compensation manager and resolved within 5 business days. You will receive an email notification upon resolution.
